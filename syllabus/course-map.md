# Course Map

## Part I: Variational And Hamiltonian Mechanics

1. Configuration spaces and generalized coordinates.
2. Action principles and Euler-Lagrange equations.
3. Constraints, multipliers, and generalized forces.
4. Symmetry and Noether's theorem.
5. Legendre transform and Hamilton's equations.
6. Phase space, symplectic form, and Poisson brackets.

## Part II: Integrability

7. Constants of motion and involution.
8. Liouville integrability.
9. Action-angle variables.
10. Kepler problem.
11. Euler top and 3D rigid-body motion.

## Part III: Perturbation And Chaos

12. Near-integrable Hamiltonians.
13. Resonance and small divisors.
14. Standard map and area-preserving maps.
15. Separatrices and chaotic layers.
16. Restricted three-body problem.
17. Asteroid-belt ejection probability experiment.

## Part IV: Continua, Fluids, And Deforming Bodies

18. Deformation maps and strain.
19. Material versus spatial descriptions.
20. Ideal fluids, vorticity, and circulation.
21. Hamiltonian point vortices.
22. Local frames, connections, and gauge freedom.
23. Cosserat rods and media.
24. Defects, curvature, and torsion.

## Recurring Computational Labs

- Euler top.
- Hamiltonian pendulum.
- Standard map.
- Sun-Jupiter-asteroid restricted problem.
- Point-vortex fluid model.
- Planar Cosserat rod reconstruction.
